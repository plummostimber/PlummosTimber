FAQ

Common Questions

Do you ship Australia-wide?

Yes — all products ship Australia-wide via registered post. Furniture pieces are quoted separately due to freight requirements.

How long do custom orders take?

Pens & small items: 1–2 weeks. Furniture & large commissions: 4–12 weeks. We'll give you a firm timeline at enquiry.

Can I choose my timber species?

Absolutely. For custom orders we discuss species availability and help you choose the right timber for your piece's use and aesthetic.

Do you offer wholesale?

Yes — we work with quality Australian retailers. Use the contact form and select "Wholesale / Stockist Enquiry" to start a conversation.
