Shipping Rates

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. We offer free standard shipping on orders over $150 Australia-wide.

Furniture & Large Items

Custom furniture pieces require freight delivery and are quoted separately. We'll contact you directly after ordering to arrange a suitable delivery method and time.

Tracking

All parcels are sent with tracking. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order is dispatched. Signature on delivery is required for orders over $100.

International Shipping

We currently ship within Australia only. International shipping may be available for small items — please contact us directly to discuss.
