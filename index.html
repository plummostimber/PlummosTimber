About Plummo's Timber

Where Nature
Meets Craft

At Plummo's Timber, we believe the finest creations aren't manufactured — they're unearthed through patience and deep respect for the natural world. As a family-run workshop, we exist at the intersection of bespoke furniture and sculptural wood art.

We hand-select every piece of timber for its unique grain, structural integrity, and the natural history it holds. No project is too small — we just enjoy the creative process.

Wood & Resin
Fusion

Our expertise in resin-infused furniture allows us to stabilise natural voids and enhance the wood's organic beauty — resulting in striking, durable designs that blur the line between art and function.

  • ▸River tables with high-clarity resin channels
  • ▸Live edge preservation and void filling
  • ▸Custom colour pigmentation and luminescent finishes
  • ▸Wall art and sculptural gallery pieces
