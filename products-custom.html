Custom Orders

No project is too small — we just enjoy the creative process. A single personalised pen, a set of wedding gifts, or a custom river table. You describe it, we build it.

What We Can Customise

Timber Species
Huon Pine, Spotted Gum, Red Mallee Burl, Camphor Laurel, Sheoak, Banksia and more
Dimensions
Any size — we build to your exact measurements and requirements
Finish
Gloss CA lacquer, satin oil, natural wax, food-safe mineral oil
Resin Colour
Any colour including glow-in-the-dark and metallic pigments
Engraving
Names, dates, logos and messages on pens, boards and furniture
Hardware
Chrome, gunmetal, gold or black titanium fittings — your choice

How It Works

01
Contact Us
Fill in the contact form or email us with your idea. The more detail the better — sketches, dimensions, timber preferences, budget.
02
We Discuss
We respond within 1–2 business days with questions, suggestions and a detailed quote.
03
Approve & Deposit
Once happy with the scope, you approve the quote and pay a 50% deposit to begin.
04
We Build
We keep you updated throughout the build with photos and progress notes at key stages.
05
Delivered
Final payment on completion. We ship Australia-wide with full tracking, or arrange pickup.
