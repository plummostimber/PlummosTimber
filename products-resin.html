Signature Technique

Wood & Resin Art

High-clarity casting resin meets Australia's finest native timbers. Our resin work preserves the organic beauty of the wood while adding a layer of modern, striking drama — gallery pieces, wall art, serving boards and river tables.

How We Work

The Resin Process

01

Timber slab prepared, dried and all voids cleaned out

02

Pigment mixed into UV-stabilised casting resin at precise ratios

03

Slow controlled pour in a temperature-controlled environment

04

72-hour cure, then flatten, sand to 12,000 grit and hand-polish

About UV Resistance

All resin work uses UV-stabilised casting resin to resist yellowing. For pieces in prolonged direct sunlight we recommend a UV-protective topcoat — ask us about this when ordering.
